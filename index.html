Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 7, 2026

👋 Overview

sNotes is a simple, offline-first notes app. Your notes stay on your device. We do not run any servers, and we do not sell or share your personal data.

This policy explains what data is collected through the third-party services we use (Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics), and what permissions the app requests.

📱 Data Stored on Your Device

All notes, titles, and attached photos are stored locally on your device only using a private SQLite database. This data is:

  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Eligible for Google's automatic device backup (optional, controlled by your Android settings)

📷 Permissions

sNotes requests the following permissions:

  • Camera — to capture photos and attach them to notes

Photos from your gallery are selected using the Android system photo picker, which requires no additional permissions.

Photos are saved to your device only. We do not upload, process, or access them for any other purpose.

📊 Firebase Analytics

Google Service

sNotes uses Firebase Analytics by Google to understand how the app is used in general (e.g. session length, screen views). This helps us improve the app.

Firebase Analytics may automatically collect:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • General location (country/region)
  • Session duration and usage patterns

No note content is ever sent to Firebase. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

📢 Google AdMob (Advertising)

Google Service

sNotes displays ads provided by Google AdMob to support free access to the app. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (can be reset in your device settings)
  • Device information
  • General location (country/region)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is used to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Privacy → Ads. For more information, see Google's Advertising Policies.

👶 Children's Privacy

sNotes is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
