Your Data Stays on Your Device

All notes, titles, and attached photos are stored locally in a private database on your device. We have no servers and no way to access your notes.

  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Note text is eligible for Google's automatic device backup — attached photos are excluded from backup

Permissions

Camera To capture photos and attach them to your notes.
Internet To fetch daily inspirational quotes and display ads via Google AdMob.
Storage (Android 8 and below only) Required to save camera photos on older devices. Not requested on Android 9 and above.

Gallery photos are selected via the Android system photo picker — no storage permission needed.

Photos are saved to your device only. We never upload, process, or share them.

Firebase Analytics

Google Service

We use Firebase Analytics to understand general usage patterns — things like session length and screen views — so we can improve the app over time.

It may collect:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • General location (country / region)
  • Session duration and usage patterns

Your note content is never sent to Firebase. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Google AdMob

Google Service

sNotes shows ads through Google AdMob to keep the app free. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (resettable in your device settings)
  • Device information
  • General location (country / region)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

You can opt out of personalised ads under Settings → Privacy → Ads on your device. See Google's Advertising Policies.

Children's Privacy

sNotes is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the app and shared personal information, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to check back periodically.
